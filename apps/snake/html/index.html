How to play:

Controls: Arrow keys to move, space to pause

Classic Snake, but with a twist. Regular food is green and grows the snake by 1. The twist is there is a rarer special type of food that is purple. Instead of growing the snake, it makes that part of the snake safe to eat as well! Eating the edible parts of the snake shortens the length of the snake and gives a much higher score.

Scoring:

  • Regular Food: 1 points
  • Special Food: 1 points
  • Edible Snake: 5 points
